The Story of Storybook

Joel is a creative consultant and videographer with 8 years in the wedding business. With a fun storytelling style and an eye for small details, he loves every moment of creating unique, vibrant and exciting wedding content.

Rachel is a writer and photographer who brings her own keen eye to the table. She sees things others miss and balances Joel's creative flare perfectly. They got married in 2022 and have been a dynamic photo/video duo ever since.

Together, they are a powerhouse of creativity and fun, passionate about creating magical memories that are everlasting. Storybook Weddings is under the umbrella of Tempero Creative, covering a wide range of digital content and creative solutions.

Common Questions

Most of our highlights videos range from 3-8 minutes. It often depends on how long we are there for the day and how many details you would like us to include. As of 2025 we have stopped selling specific length products, as we were often gutted to be cutting out awesome shots from the day.

Music licensing can be a tricky thing. We endeavour to accommodate ideas wherever possible, but sometimes it just won't be possible. We will work this through with you during our initial discussions - we love having the couple be a part of our creative process!

A full day shoot means we're there when the girls start getting their hair and makeup done and we finish when we feel we have enough of the evening dancing. A half day typically looks like either morning prep + ceremony or ceremony + reception. If you're after just a ceremony shoot this will still be considered a half day.

For sure! Travel fees will apply for anything outside of Christchurch. We can quote for this on request. Send us anywhere you want us to be!

Typically Joel is shooting video and Rachel is on photography. If you order just video or photo then we will both cover it for you on the day. Sometimes we may swap for specific moments or ideas. We're happy to report that we can be quite flexible!

A deposit of at least 1/3rd of your overall quote will be required after booking. This is to protect us from any sudden cancellations.
